GHATS

Kashi Ghats, lining the sacred Ganga River in Varanasi, showcase spiritual vibrancy, ancient rituals, and breathtaking views. From Assi to Dashashwamedh, they symbolize India's deep cultural and religious heritage.

SARNATH

Sarnath, located near Varanasi, is a revered Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon. It is home to iconic landmarks like the Dhamek Stupa, Ashoka Pillar, and Sarnath Museum, showcasing India's rich spiritual heritage.
